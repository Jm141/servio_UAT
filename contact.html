Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Servio different from other software companies?

We're countryside-born and understand the unique challenges of rural digital transformation. Our solutions are built specifically for Philippine LGUs, cooperatives, and SMEs, with local support and compliance built-in.

How long does implementation typically take?

Implementation timelines vary by project scope. Simple LGU systems can be ready in 2-3 months, while comprehensive ERP solutions may take 6-12 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our discovery call.

Do you provide training and support?

Yes! We offer comprehensive training for your team, ongoing technical support, and regular updates. Our goal is to make you self-sufficient while providing backup support when needed.

What about data security and compliance?

We prioritize Philippine data sovereignty and compliance. Our solutions can be deployed on-premise or in local data centers, ensuring your data stays within Philippine borders and meets government requirements.

Can you customize solutions for our specific needs?

Absolutely! We specialize in customizing ERPNext and other platforms to match your exact requirements. Whether it's LGU-specific workflows or cooperative member management, we'll tailor the solution to your needs.

What if we need ongoing development?

We offer ongoing development services, maintenance contracts, and can train your local IT team. We're committed to long-term partnerships that grow with your organization.
