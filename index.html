Our Story

SERVIO TECHNOLOGIES

SERVIO Technologies is a proudly Filipino-owned IT company founded in 2019 in Roxas City, Capiz, with a clear mission: to make digital solutions simple, smart, and affordable for small and medium-sized businesses in the Philippines.

Backed by major investors like Mega Global Corporation and Jadel Holdings, SERVIO has grown rapidly, offering a full suite of business tools that include payroll, HR, accounting, point-of-sale systems, cybersecurity, and enterprise-level software‚Äîeverything a modern business needs to thrive in the digital age.
